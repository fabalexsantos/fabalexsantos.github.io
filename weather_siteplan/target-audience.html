Target Audience

Target Audience

The site can be used by anyone planning to practice outdoor sports, such as fishing, football, hiking, climbing and many others. Therefore, teenagers, parents, physical education teachers, coaches will benefit from the information contained on the website.

Personas

Jhon Rich He would like to go fishing with his family on the weekend. He feels a little insecure, as he fears that the weather is rainy and that would hinder his hobby. In addition, Jhon has young children and would not like to face serious weather-related problems. His wife does not like fishing, and accompanying him on a rainy day could wear out his relationship. For those reasons, the site can help Jhon to have a good experience on his weekend.

Margaret Simons is a principal at a high school. She would like the physical education teacher to create a small volleyball tournament with the students. From her experience as a director, Margaret knows that sunny days will bring more people to watch the tournament. In the last school event there was a lot of rain and therefore few people attended. Through the website, Margaret can consult the days of greatest probability of sun, increasing the chances of success of her tournament.

Scenarios

I want to go camping for the weekend. Will it be sunny?

The futsal tournament final is tomorrow! It's cloudy. Will it rain?

Saturday will be a good day to plan my fishing.

Next week I am going to walk in the forest. The weather needs to be with because the rain disrupts these activities too much.

I'm going to schedule a game for my volleyball team. I would like a sunny day, since the public will attend in greater numbers.
